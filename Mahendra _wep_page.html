MAHENDRA ENGINEERING COLLEGE FOR WOMEN

Mahendra Engineering College for Women was established by the Mahendra Educational Trust, led by the esteemed educationalist Shri. M.G. Bharathkumar, who serves as the Trust’s Chairman and is a renowned philanthropist in the region. Recognizing the significance of technical education for women, the Mahendra Educational Trust inaugurated their first college exclusively for women, Mahendra Engineering College for Women, in 2008. The college is located in Kumaramangalam, Tiruchengode, Namakkal District. It holds approval from AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) and is affiliated with Anna University Chennai. The institution offers four undergraduate programs and one post graduate program in the field of Engineering and Technology.
